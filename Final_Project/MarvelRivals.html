Introduction

Spider-Man is one of the most fascinating characters and likely has one of the highest skill gaps in the game. In this guide, I’ll go over his abilities and provide some basic information to help you understand his playstyle. Let’s get started!

Basic Controls

Before I get started, I want to give credit most of this information comes from the amazing Spider-Man playbook made by the community and to one of the best Spider-Man players, Necros. To begin, Spider-Man has two main abilities you would use: his SPIDER-POWER attacks and his web-clusters. Web clusters boost and make the rest of his kit stronger and more useful. His shift ability, web swing, is the classic movement that Spidey comes with.

Combos

Spidey has a lot of combos but I will be going over the two most basic ones: the one with Venom team-up and the one without. For the one with the Venom team-up, you will shoot a web, then web-zip to the person, and then uppercut into a web shot, then jump and melee downslam. now the video i provied below shows a lot of combos and you can also look them up with this link
