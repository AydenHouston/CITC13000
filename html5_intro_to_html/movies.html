The break down of the movies in the spiderman trilogy

The Spider-Man trilogy in the MCU consists of three films, each playing a crucial role in Peter Parker's journey. These movies not only showcase Spider-Man's growth but also his relationship with other Marvel characters and the broader cinematic universe

Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017)

Peter Parker, under the mentorship of Tony Stark, tries to balance high school life while taking on the villainous Vulture. This film introduces a younger, more relatable version of Spider-Man.

Spider-Man: Far From Home (2019)

Following the events of Avengers: Endgame, Peter embarks on a European trip but faces unexpected threats from Mysterio, who manipulates him using advanced illusions.
