Introduction

Spider-Man has appeared in various TV shows over the years, each bringing a unique take on the beloved character. From animated series to live-action adaptations, these shows have explored different aspects of Spider-Man's life and adventures.

Animated Series

Spider-Man: The Animated Series (1994) is one of the most iconic adaptations, known for its faithful representation of the comic book storylines. Other notable animated series include The Spectacular Spider-Man (2008) and Ultimate Spider-Man (2012).

Live-Action Series

Spider-Man has also been featured in live-action series, such as The Amazing Spider-Man (1977) and the more recent Marvel's Spider-Man (2017). These shows have brought the web-slinger to life in new and exciting ways.
